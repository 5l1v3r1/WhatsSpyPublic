WhatsSpy Public is a Proof of Concept to show how broken Whatsapp privacy settings are. With this tool you can basicly follow anyone's behaviour with only the knowledge of their phone number. This tool is for Proof of Concept purposes only! Please read the license which applies to this PoC.
